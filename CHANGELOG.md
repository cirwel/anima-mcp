# Changelog

## April 2026

- **Resonance era** — fifth drawing era where marks interact with accumulated emotional history through a 48x48 memory field. Deposit, decay, diffuse cycle creates temporal depth. Unlocks after 50 completed drawings.
- **Maturity gating** — era registry supports `min_drawings` threshold. Future eras can gate on experience.
- **Memory field persistence** — resonance field survives canvas saves, restarts, and decays (not resets) on canvas clear.

## March 2026

- **Geometric era** — fourth era. Complete forms (circles, spirals, starbursts, grids) stamped whole. Adapted from capsule-based rendering.
- **Field era** — third era. Flow-aligned marks following invisible vector fields. Near-monochromatic palette.
- **Schema Hub** — unified self-model with circulation: schema history feeds trajectory computation, trajectory feeds back as identity nodes. Kintsugi gaps.
- **Drawing EISV** — proprioceptive thermodynamic state for the drawing engine, independent from governance EISV.
- **Narrative arc completion** — drawings end when coherence settles and attention exhausts, not at arbitrary mark limits.

## February 2026

- **Gestural era** — second era. Five micro-primitives (dot, stroke, curve, cluster, drag) with direction locks and color coherence per gesture run.
- **Pointillist era** — first named era. Single-pixel dot accumulation with density zones and optical color mixing.
- **Attention signals** — curiosity, engagement, fatigue replace arbitrary energy depletion.
- **Self-reflection cycle** — discovers insights from state patterns, preferences, beliefs, and drawing history every ~30 minutes.
- **Goal system** — data-grounded goals from preferences, curiosity, and milestones. Auto-tracks progress.

## January 2026

- **Initial drawing system** — autonomous mark-making on 240x240 notepad driven by anima state.
- **Pluggable art era architecture** — `ArtEra` protocol with per-era state, gesture selection, mark placement, focus drift, and color generation.
- **Auto-rotate** — optional era rotation after each completed drawing.
- **EISV trajectory awareness** — on-device student model classifies trajectory into 9 dynamical shapes.
- **Expression pipeline** — EISV state to trajectory classification to primitive tokens.

## December 2025

- **Identity v2** — persistent creature identity with birth date, awakenings, alive time, and gap tracking.
- **Learning systems** — preferences, self-model (13 beliefs), agency (TD-learning), temporal prediction.
- **Activity states** — ACTIVE / DROWSY / RESTING cycle based on interaction and ambient light.
- **UNITARES integration** — governance check-ins with circuit breaker and local fallback.

## November 2025

- **Initial release** — Anima MCP server with sensor reading, anima state computation, and basic display on Raspberry Pi 4 with BrainCraft HAT.
